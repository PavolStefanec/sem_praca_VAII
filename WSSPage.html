While She Sleeps


Bio

While She Sleeps are a British metalcore band from Sheffield. Band formed in 2006. They received the Best British Newcomer award at the Kerrang! Awards 2012. The group has maintained the same line-up since its formation, with vocalist Taylor being the only bandmate who is not an original member. Despite this, he has still appeared on every release apart from the group's first two EPs.


Active members

Sean Long – lead guitar, backing vocals (2006–present)
Mat Welsh – rhythm guitar, piano, vocals (2006–present)
Lawrence Taylor – lead vocals (2009–present)
Aaran McKenzie – bass, backing vocals (2006–present)
Adam Savage – drums, percussion (2006–present)
